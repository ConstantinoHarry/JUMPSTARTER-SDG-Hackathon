Problem Statement

The increasing prevalence of mental illnesses, society’s level of acceptance toward individuals with mental health needs remains insufficient, while prejudices, negative stigmas, and negative media reporting continue to persist.

  • 2020: >250,000 mental issues affected (3.4% prevalence)
  • 2019–20: 270,000 → 2023–24: 300,000 seeking care (+11%)
  • Stigma delays help across children, adults, elderly

(Equal Opportunities Commission, 2024)

Target Users

  • Children (10+) with school stress
  • Adults (work/family pressure)
  • Elderly (loneliness, cognitive decline)
